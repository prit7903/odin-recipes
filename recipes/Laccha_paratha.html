Laccha Paratha

Description

Laccha Paratha is very delicious and popular with Paneer sabji or chole bhature.

Ingrdients

  • og ye kya hai
  • Hame to sirf matlab hai
  • Khaane se, Haan ji!

Steps

  1. First cut the piece using first two fingers & thumb.
  2. Pour around half part in sabji, make sure to take a little bit bite of paneer or chola.
  3. Enjoy the taste!
